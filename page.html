how to make tea

At the very least, tea /hot chocolate is a flavourful way of getting enough fluid into your body each day. On top of that, studies have shown teas can help protect your teeth and your heart

ingredients

  • Tea Bag
  • Sugar/Honey
  • Water
  • Milk

instructions

  1. Run a clean water,boil it once to maintain high oxygen level
  2. Pop a tea bag or hot chocolate in the mug always BIG CUP
  3. Put some hot water and stir it up
  4. Before removing the tea bag, gently squish it with a spoon against the side of the mug. Not too much or you'll make it bitter
  5. If you want, throw in some milk or sugar or honey or nothing else at all.
